## About
### Supported environments:
- GNOME
- Xfce
- Cinnamon
- Budgie
- COSMIC (Pop!_OS)
- Pantheon
- MATE
- KDE Plasma

### SaveDesktop can save:
- your icons and themes
- your settings
- your backgrounds (including dynamic wallpapers, provided that the same username is retained)
- your GNOME and Nautilus extensions

and more...
